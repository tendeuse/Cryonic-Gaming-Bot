# EVE Online Corporation Military Structure

**Roles, Promotions, Branches, and Fleet Alignment**

---

## 1. Rank → EVE Role & Permission Mapping

> Principle: **Least privilege required**, authority increases by tier, wallet access only where operationally necessary.

### Member (Line Pilot)

**EVE Permissions**

* Member role only
* Access to:

  * Corp chat
  * Designated hangars (read / limited take)
  * Corp bookmarks (view)
* No wallet access
* No structure management

**Purpose**

* Fly, learn, participate
* Zero administrative risk

---

### Officer (Squad Leader)

**EVE Permissions**

* Junior Accountant *(optional, branch-dependent)*
* Hangar: take from squad-level hangars
* Access to:

  * Fleet creation
  * Corp bookmarks (create, limited)
* No structure or wallet division control

**Purpose**

* Lead squads
* Handle daily operations
* Act as first escalation layer

---

### Commander (Company Leader)

**EVE Permissions**

* Accountant (limited wallet division)
* Hangar: full access to operational hangars
* Structure access (use only, no config)
* Can create corp contracts (branch-relevant)
* Can assign Officers (out of game policy)

**Purpose**

* Coordinate multiple squads
* Run medium-to-large ops
* Control operational assets

---

### General (Theater Command)

**EVE Permissions**

* Senior Accountant (multiple wallet divisions)
* Hangar: full access
* Structure management (fueling, anchoring delegated)
* Contract management
* POS / Structure defense access

**Purpose**

* Strategic coordination
* Resource prioritization
* Campaign oversight

---

### Director (Branch Executive)

**EVE Permissions**

* Director role (restricted to branch scope via policy)
* Full hangar access (branch-specific)
* Full wallet access (branch divisions)
* Structure configuration (branch-owned)
* Contract creation and acceptance
* Ability to appoint Generals (policy-based)

**Purpose**

* Execute CEO strategy within a domain
* Maintain operational readiness
* Prevent CEO micromanagement

---

### Corporation Leader / CEO

**EVE Permissions**

* CEO role
* Full access to all corp systems
* Alliance-level authority

**Purpose**

* Vision, diplomacy, war declarations
* Final authority and accountability

---

## 2. Promotion Requirements (Merit-Based)

### Member → Officer

**Requirements**

* Minimum activity period (e.g. 30–60 days)
* Demonstrated reliability and comms discipline
* Basic doctrine proficiency
* Recommendation from Commander

---

### Officer → Commander

**Requirements**

* Successfully led squads
* Proven conflict resolution ability
* Consistent participation
* Understanding of corp SOPs
* Director approval (branch)

---

### Commander → General

**Requirements**

* Multi-squad coordination experience
* Campaign planning capability
* Trusted with assets
* Strategic thinking
* CEO or Director council approval

---

### General → Director

**Requirements**

* Exceptional leadership record
* Long-term availability
* Trusted with corp assets
* CEO appointment only

---

## 3. Branch-Specific Director Roles

Each Director controls **their own Generals, Commanders, Officers, and Members**.

---

### Director of Industry

**Scope**

* Mining
* Manufacturing
* Reactions
* PI

**Permissions**

* Industry wallet divisions
* Structure services (refineries, engineering)
* Blueprint libraries

**Fleet Alignment**

* Mining Foreman
* Boost fleet commander
* Industrial operation FC

---

### Director of Transport & Logistics

**Scope**

* Hauling
* Supply chains
* Asset movement

**Permissions**

* Transport wallet
* Contract authority
* Freighter / JF hangars

**Fleet Alignment**

* Logistics Wing Commander
* Hauling fleet FC
* Cyno coordination

---

### Director of Exploration

**Scope**

* Scanning
* Intel gathering
* WH mapping

**Permissions**

* Bookmark management
* Intel channels
* Scanner tool oversight

**Fleet Alignment**

* Scout Lead
* Recon Wing Commander
* Pathfinder coordinator

---

### Director of Onboarding & Training

**Scope**

* Recruitment
* Newbro training
* Mentorship

**Permissions**

* Limited hangar grants
* Training wallet
* Corp welcome assets

**Fleet Alignment**

* Training FC
* Rookie fleet commander
* Doctrine instructor

---

### Director of Combat & Wormhole Defense

**Scope**

* PvP

**Fleet Alignment**

## 4. Alignment with Fleet Command Structure

This hierarchy maps **perfectly** to EVE’s fleet system.

| Corp Rank | Fleet Role                        |
| --------- | --------------------------------- |
| Member    | Line Pilot                        |
| Officer   | Squad Commander                   |
| Commander | Wing Commander                    |
| General   | Fleet Commander / Strategic FC    |
| Director  | Theater FC / Doctrine Authority   |
| CEO       | Strategic Command / War Authority |

### Fleet Example

```
Fleet Commander (General / Director)
└── Wing Commanders (Commanders)
    └── Squad Commanders (Officers)
        └── Line Pilots (Members)
```
