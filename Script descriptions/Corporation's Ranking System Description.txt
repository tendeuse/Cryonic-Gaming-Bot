---

## 1. Member (Line Pilot)

**Role:** Operational personnel
**Reports to:** Officer
**Ratio:** Up to 10 Members per Officer

### Responsibilities

* Participate in assigned operations (PvP, PvE, Industry, Logistics)
* Follow fleet commands and corporate doctrine
* Maintain operational readiness
* Escalate issues or requests through their Officer

---

## 2. Officer (Squad Leader)

**Role:** First-line leadership
**Reports to:** Commander
**Ratio:** Up to 10 Officers per Commander
**Manages:** Up to 100 Members indirectly

### Responsibilities

* Lead and mentor Members
* Execute small-scale operations
* Enforce standards and discipline
* Report readiness and issues upward

---

## 3. Commander (Company Leader)

**Role:** Operational command
**Reports to:** General
**Ratio:** Up to 10 Commanders per General
**Manages:** Up to 1,000 Members indirectly

### Responsibilities

* Coordinate multiple Officers
* Plan and execute large-scale operations
* Maintain cohesion across squads
* Provide operational feedback to Generals

---

## 4. General (Theater Command)

**Role:** Strategic oversight
**Reports to:** Director
**Ratio:** Up to 10 Generals per Director
**Manages:** Up to 10,000 Members indirectly

### Responsibilities

* Oversee multiple operational domains
* Set strategic objectives and priorities
* Allocate resources and manpower
* Advise Directors on strategic risks and opportunities

---

## 5. Director (Executive Command)

**Role:** Corporate executive leadership
**Reports to:** Corporation Leader / CEO
**Number:** **Exactly 5 Directors**

### Responsibilities

* Oversee entire operational branches (e.g., PvP, Industry, Logistics, Intelligence, Training)
* Appoint and remove Generals within their branch
* Translate corporate strategy into executable objectives
* Ensure cross-branch coordination

### Purpose

Directors act as **executive commanders**, bridging strategy and execution while preventing micromanagement from the CEO.

---

## 6. Corporation Leader / CEO

**Role:** Supreme authority
**Reports to:** None
**Direct Reports:** 5 Directors

### Responsibilities

* Define corporate vision, culture, and long-term goals
* Appoint and remove Directors
* Approve wars, alliances, mergers, and major economic decisions
* Represent the corporation diplomatically at alliance and coalition levels

### Purpose

The CEO ensures **unity of command**, strategic consistency, and long-term corporate survival.

---

## Full Command Hierarchy (Summary)

```
Corporation Leader / CEO
└── 5 Directors
    └── Up to 10 Generals each
        └── Up to 10 Commanders each
            └── Up to 10 Officers each
                └── Up to 10 Members each
```
